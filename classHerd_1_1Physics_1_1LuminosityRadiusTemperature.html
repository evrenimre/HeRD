Detailed Description

Relation between the electromagnetic radiation emitted by a blackbody, its temperature and radius.

Remarks
Expressions
  • The equation is \( L = 4\pi\sigma_{SB}R^2T^4 \) where \( \sigma_{SB} \) is the Stefan-Boltzmann constant
  • Expressed in terms of solar units, it becomes \( \frac{L}{L_{\odot}} = \left( \frac{R}{R_{\odot}} \right)^2 \left( \frac{T}{T_{\odot}} \right)^4 \)
